## Summary
Pawductivity is a **local-first** app. Your tasks, your companion, your progress, and your
settings are stored **only on your device**. We do **not** run servers, we do **not** have
accounts, and we do **not** collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

## Information we collect
**None on our side.** Pawductivity has no user accounts and no backend. All content you create —
tasks/quests, focus history, reminders, your pet, coins, and settings — is saved locally on your
device using on-device storage. It is not sent to us or to any third party by the app.

We do not collect names, email addresses, contacts, location, photos, device identifiers for
tracking, or usage analytics.

## Permissions we use, and why
- **Notifications** — to show reminders you set and to alert you when a focus session finishes.
  Notification content is generated on your device from the reminders/timers you create.
- **Alarms & reminders / exact alarm** — to fire reminders and focus-completion alerts at the
  precise time you chose.
- **Run at startup (boot)** — to re-schedule your pending reminders after your device restarts, so
  they still fire.
- **Prevent sleeping / wake lock** — to keep the screen awake while a focus timer is on screen.

None of these permissions send data off your device.

## Purchases
If you buy an optional Premium subscription (or other in-app products), the purchase is processed
by **Google Play Billing**. We never see or store your payment details. Google's handling of that
transaction is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy and Play terms. Your entitlement (whether
Premium is active) is stored locally on your device.

## Data sharing
We do not share your data with anyone, because the app does not collect it. There is no
advertising SDK and no analytics SDK in the app.

## Data retention and deletion
Because your data lives only on your device, **you control it entirely**. You can delete all of it
at any time from within the app (a reset/clear-data option) or by uninstalling the app. There is
no server-side copy for us to delete, and no account to close.

## Children's privacy
Pawductivity does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.
Because no data is collected or transmitted, the app is suitable for a general audience. If you
believe a child has provided personal information through some external channel, contact us and we
will assist — though the app itself stores nothing off-device.

## Optional AI features (if enabled in a future version)
The current version processes everything on your device with no network calls for its features. If
a future version adds an optional AI assistant that requires sending text to a third-party AI
provider, it will be **off by default**, clearly disclosed at the point of use, and this policy
will be updated to describe exactly what is sent and to whom before that feature ships.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest
version. Material changes will be reflected here (and, where appropriate, in the app).
